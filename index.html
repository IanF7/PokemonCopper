About Pokémon Copper

Background

Pokémon Copper is a fan-made Pokémon game created using Pokémon Essentials v21.1. The game takes place in the Azori Region, a region inspired by the U.S. state of Arizona.

While the Azori Region uses the familiar route structure of traditional Pokémon games, it introduces a more open progression system. After the first gym, players are free to challenge the remaining gyms and experience much of the story in the order they choose.

Trainers and wild Pokémon scale with the player as they progress, with a few special exceptions, allowing for a more flexible and personalized adventure.

Pokémon

The Azori Region features 113 brand-new Pokémon, 10 regional variants, 10 new Mega Evolutions for returning Pokémon, and 12 Mega Evolutions for new Azori Pokémon.

The game also includes 47 new moves, some of which are exclusive to the new Pokémon, along with 9 new abilities.

The Azori Pokédex contains a total of 500 Pokémon, combining both new and returning species. There are also rewards for completing the Pokédex, so there is plenty of reason to catch as many Pokémon as possible.

The Story

Pokémon Copper features a full Pokémon-style story. The player begins in their hometown of Terah Town, receives their starter Pokémon from Professor Verde, meets their rival, Ewan, and then sets out on a journey to collect all 8 gym badges.

Along the way, the player must also deal with a criminal organization known as Team Eclipse. After earning all 8 gym badges, the player enters the Horizon Cup tournament, where they compete alongside 8 other trainers for the title of Champion.

Once that title is earned, the player gains the right to face the Elite 4 and the Pokémon League Champion. The game also includes smaller post-game story events involving two new Mythical Pokémon and the newly constructed Azori Terrarium, where players can catch the remaining Pokémon not found in the base game.

Features

  • 113 New Pokémon
  • 10 New Regional Variants
  • 10 New Mega Evolutions for returning Pokémon
  • 12 Mega Evolutions for Azori Pokémon
  • Regional Dex with 500 total Pokémon
  • Open-world progression
  • Custom day/night cycle
  • Full Pokémon-style story and post-game content
  • Level scaling for trainers and wild Pokémon based on badge count and party levels
  • Light character customization
  • Autosave
  • In-depth Pokédex information, including evolution methods
  • EXP All and EXP Candies in Poké Marts to reduce grinding
  • PokéSummons that replace HMs
